About Pebble

The story behind the brand

Pebble was born from a simple frustration: great audio shouldn't require a second mortgage. In 2021, Drew Gallagher realized that the gap between $20 disposables and $500 "audiophile" earbuds was wide enough to drive a truck through — so he built a bridge.

Every Pebble product is engineered in Brooklyn and manufactured with partners who share our obsession with fit, finish, and frequency response. We don't believe in annual refreshes. We believe in getting it right and leaving it alone until we can genuinely improve it. Our drivers are custom-tuned on a Klippel analyzer. Our cables are braided by hand. Our charging cases are designed to feel like a river stone — hence the name.

Today, Pebble is carried by select independent vendors who share our philosophy: sell less, sell better. You won't find us in big-box stores. You'll find us on the shelves of people who actually listen.
