Sustainability

Our commitment to a slower, more responsible way of making and buying — because the most sustainable products are the ones that last.

At Lahar, sustainability isn't a campaign — it's the reason we exist. We believe the most sustainable products are the ones that last, the ones with a story, and the ones that never end up in a landfill. From day one, every decision we've made has been guided by that belief.

What we do

Three pillars that guide our approach to sustainability every day.

Made to last

We curate for durability, not disposability. Every product is chosen for its quality of materials, construction, and timeless design. We prioritise natural fibres, solid wood, stainless steel, and other materials that age gracefully.

Carbon offset

Every shipment on Lahar is carbon offset through verified reforestation and renewable energy projects. We partner with ClimatePartner to calculate and offset the full lifecycle emissions of every order — including last-mile delivery.

Minimal packaging

We encourage and support our vendors to use recycled, compostable, and plastic-free packaging. Our own shipping materials are 100% recycled cardboard, and all labels are carbon neutral. We also offset the packaging footprint.

Our approach

We prioritise vendors who use sustainable materials, ethical production methods, and traditional craftsmanship. Made-to-order and small-batch production means less waste, fewer unsold goods, and a smaller environmental footprint.

Vintage and restored goods give existing products a second life. We're building a marketplace where repair, restoration, and longevity are celebrated — not hidden behind marketing jargon.

We measure our impact transparently and publish an annual sustainability report. No greenwashing, no vague promises — just honest data about where we are and where we need to go.

Our commitments

  • 100% carbon neutral shipping by 2026
  • Zero plastic in all Lahar-branded packaging
  • Annual transparency report with third-party audit
  • 1% of revenue donated to reforestation projects
  • Maker toolkit for sustainable packaging transition
