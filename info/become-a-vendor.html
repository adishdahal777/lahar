Frequently asked questions
Everything you need to know about selling on Lahar.
How much does it cost to sell on Lahar?

You can start for free with our Starter plan — no monthly fee, 8% commission. Most vendors choose Standard ($19/mo, 5% commission). Premium ($49/mo, 3%) and Enterprise ($99/mo, <2%) are available for established brands. No setup fees, no hidden charges.

How and when do I get paid?

Payouts are triggered 24 hours after delivery confirmation. Standard plan vendors can schedule weekly or bi-weekly payouts. Premium and Enterprise vendors get instant withdrawal to their bank account or PayPal. Minimum payout: $10.

Do I need to handle shipping myself?

You pack and ship your products — we provide discounted shipping labels through our carrier network (UPS, FedEx, USPS, DHL). The label cost is deducted from the buyer's payment so you never pay out of pocket. We also offer an optional pick-and-pack service in select regions.

Who handles customer support and returns?

We handle first-line customer support, including returns and disputes. Our 30-day free returns policy is fully managed by Lahar — we provide the label, process the refund, and cover any losses. You just need to accept the return when it arrives. Premium and Enterprise vendors get white-glove dispute resolution.

Can I sell internationally?

Absolutely. Lahar ships to over 60 countries and we handle all cross-border customs documentation automatically. Vendors can set different pricing and shipping rules per region. We also offer multi-currency payouts in USD, EUR, JPY, GBP, and DKK.

What kind of products can I sell?

We accept handmade, designed, curated, and vintage goods across fashion, home, electronics, ceramics, jewellery, art, furniture, and more. Every product is reviewed before going live to ensure quality and authenticity. We do not accept mass-produced, drop-shipped, or counterfeit items.
