Measuring tips

01

Chest / Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the measuring tape horizontal and snug but not tight. Breathe normally.

02

Waist

Measure at the natural waist — the narrowest part of your torso, typically just above the belly button. Keep the tape comfortably loose.

03

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips and bottom, about 20cm below your natural waist. Stand with feet together.

04

Inseam

Measure from the crotch seam to the bottom of the leg hem on a pair of pants that fit you well. Keep the pants flat on the floor.
