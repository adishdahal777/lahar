About Lahar

Lahar is a Copenhagen-based marketplace connecting independent makers with buyers who value quality, provenance, and story. Founded in 2019 by Ravi Mehta and Aiko Tanaka, Lahar curates fashion, home, electronics, and lifestyle products from 320+ vetted vendors across 42 countries.

We are a remote-first team of 12 people spread across Copenhagen, Tokyo, and Melbourne. Our mission is to build a marketplace that works for makers — with low commissions (5%), fair terms, and a curation process that prioritises craftsmanship over volume.
