Orders & shipping

How long does shipping take?

Dispatch times vary by vendor and are shown on each product page. Most ready-to-ship items go out within 1–5 business days. Domestic delivery typically takes 2–5 days after dispatch; international 5–14 days depending on customs.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email. You can also track all your orders from your account dashboard under Order History.

Do you ship internationally?

We ship to over 60 countries. Duties and taxes are calculated at checkout. Some vendors may have regional restrictions — these will be noted on the product page.

Returns & refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer free returns within 30 days of delivery on most items. Simply initiate a return from your order page, print the label, and drop off the package. Refunds are processed within 5 business days of the vendor receiving the item.

What items are non-returnable?

Personalised, made-to-order, and final sale items are non-returnable unless defective. Vintage items must be returned in original condition. See our full Shipping & Returns page for details.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, PayPal, and select local payment methods depending on your region. All payments are processed securely through Stripe or PayPal.

When is my card charged?

Your card is charged when you place the order. Funds are held securely and released to the vendor only after they confirm shipment. If the vendor doesn't ship within the promised window, you'll receive a full automatic refund.

Accounts

How do I create an account?

Click "Create account" at the top of any page. You can sign up with your email or using Google, Apple, or Facebook. It's free and takes less than a minute.

I forgot my password — what do I do?

Visit the password reset page, enter your email, and we'll send you a reset link.
