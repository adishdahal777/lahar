Why Lahar?

We're a small, remote-first team spread across Copenhagen, Tokyo, and Melbourne. We value craftsmanship in everything we do — from the code we write to the relationships we build.

Remote-first

Work from anywhere in the world. We coordinate asynchronously across time zones and meet up twice a year as a team. Your home office budget is on us.

Slow pace

No crunch, no burnout. We work at a sustainable pace, ship thoughtfully, and respect each other's time and boundaries. Four-day work weeks in summer.

Real impact

Your work directly supports 320+ independent makers and a growing community of conscious buyers. Every feature ships, every decision matters.

Life at Lahar

We don't have a headquarters — our office is wherever you are. Our team spans 8 cities and 4 time zones, connected by a shared belief that commerce can be slower, kinder, and more meaningful.

We communicate mostly async through written documents and recorded updates. We run on a 4-day week during July and August. Every quarter, we cover travel for the team to meet in person somewhere beautiful.

"The best part of working at Lahar is that you're never a cog in a machine. Your voice actually shapes the direction of the company."
