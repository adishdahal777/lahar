Shipping

Dispatch times

Each vendor sets their own dispatch window, displayed on the product page and at checkout. Estimated dispatch times typically range from 1–5 business days for ready-to-ship items. Made-to-order items may take longer, as noted in the listing. During peak seasons (Black Friday, holidays), dispatch may be delayed by 1–2 business days.

Shipping rates

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the vendor's settings, package weight, and destination. Many vendors offer free shipping on orders over a certain amount — this is clearly marked on the product page and at checkout. Lahar provides discounted rates through our carrier network, and vendors pass these savings on to you. Standard domestic shipping starts from £4.99 / €5.90.

International shipping

We ship to over 60 countries worldwide. Import duties, taxes, and customs fees are calculated and displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase. Lahar handles all customs documentation automatically — you don't need to fill out any forms. Delivery times for international orders vary by destination and customs processing, typically 5–14 business days after dispatch.

Returns

30-day free returns

If you're not completely satisfied, you may return eligible items within 30 days of delivery. Lahar provides a prepaid return label — you just need to pack the item securely and drop it off at the carrier location. There are no restocking fees and no hidden charges. We believe in risk-free shopping.

Conditions

  • Items must be unused and in their original packaging with all tags attached
  • Personalised or made-to-order items are non-returnable unless defective or damaged
  • Vintage items must be returned in the exact condition received
  • Return shipping is free for standard items; we deduct the label cost from the refund only if the item doesn't meet the conditions above
  • Footwear and apparel must be tried on indoors only — no signs of wear on soles or linings

How to return

  1. Log into your account and go to Order History, then select the order containing the item you wish to return
  2. Click "Initiate return" and select the item(s) and reason for return
  3. Print the prepaid shipping label — or simply show the QR code at the carrier's drop-off location
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging, attach the label, and drop it off
  5. Your refund is processed within 5 business days of the vendor receiving and inspecting the item

Refunds

Refunds are issued to your original payment method. Processing time depends on your bank or payment provider — typically 3–10 business days for credit cards, 1–3 days for PayPal, and immediately for store credit. If you used store credit, the refund will be returned as credit. You'll receive an email confirmation the moment your refund is initiated.

Exchanges

Exchanges are handled as a return followed by a new purchase. Return the original item for a full refund and place a new order for the desired item. This two-step process ensures accurate inventory tracking, faster processing, and avoids shipping delays. If you're exchanging due to a defect or our error, we'll cover the return shipping and provide a discount code for the replacement order.

Damaged or incorrect items

If your order arrives damaged or doesn't match its description, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery at support@laharhub.com. Include your order number and clear photos of the damage or discrepancy. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you, including return shipping if needed. Our team typically resolves these issues within 2 business days.
