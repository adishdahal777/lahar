Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Information we collect

You provide

  • Your full name and email address
  • Shipping and billing addresses
  • Payment card details (processed by our PCI-compliant partners)
  • Product listings, photos, descriptions, and reviews you upload
  • Communications you send to us or to vendors
  • Account preferences — language, currency, notification settings

Automatically

  • IP address, browser type, and operating system
  • Device identifiers and screen resolution
  • Pages viewed, time spent, and navigation path
  • Referral source (how you found Lahar)
  • Cookie and local storage data for session management
  • Interaction data — clicks, scroll depth, searches

How we use your information

Transactions

Process orders, handle payments, arrange shipping, and send order confirmations. We share only what's necessary with vendors and carriers to fulfil your purchase.

Improve

Analyse usage patterns to fix bugs, optimise performance, and refine features. Aggregate data helps us understand what our community needs and how we can serve them better.

Recommend

Suggest products, vendors, and collections tailored to your taste. We use your browsing history, past purchases, and favourites to personalise your feed without selling your data.

Payment information

Your payment data is never stored on our servers

All payment processing is handled by Stripe and PayPal, our trusted third-party payment partners. Full credit card numbers are encrypted and tokenised — we only retain the last four digits, the card brand, and an expiration month for reference. Vendor payouts are processed through Stripe Connect, subject to their respective privacy policies. Our platform is PCI DSS compliant, and we conduct quarterly security audits to maintain that certification.

Information sharing

With vendors

When you place an order, the vendor receives your name, shipping address, email, and order details — enough to fulfil and communicate about your purchase. Vendors are contractually prohibited from using this data for any other purpose.

Service providers

We engage trusted partners for payment processing, shipping and logistics, fraud detection, analytics (Google Analytics), and customer support ticketing. Each provider is vetted and bound by data processing agreements.

Legal compliance

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request. We also reserve the right to share data to protect the rights, property, or safety of Lahar, our users, or the public.

Data retention & Your rights

Data retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account remains active. If you close your account, we delete or anonymise your personal data within 90 days. Transaction records are retained for 7 years to comply with tax and legal obligations. Anonymous analytics data may be kept indefinitely.

Your rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

Security & Contact

Security measures

We implement TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit, AES-256 encryption at rest, and mandatory two-factor authentication for vendor dashboard access. Our infrastructure is monitored 24/7, and we undergo regular penetration testing. Despite these measures, no online platform is completely risk-free — please use strong passwords and enable 2FA on your account.
