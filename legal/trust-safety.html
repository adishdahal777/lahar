Trust & Safety

How we keep Lahar safe for buyers and makers.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Our commitment

Trust is the foundation of Lahar

Every vendor is vetted, every product is reviewed before going live, and every transaction is backed by our purchase protection guarantee. We built Lahar so that buyers shop with confidence and makers sell with fairness. Our trust and safety team works around the clock to enforce our policies, detect fraud, and respond to community concerns.

Vendor vetting

Every maker is reviewed before they can sell

  • 1 Application review: Every vendor application is manually reviewed by our curation team. We assess product quality, brand authenticity, and alignment with our community values.
  • 2 Business verification: Vendors must provide proof of business registration, tax identification, and a valid government-issued ID. This information is verified before any listings go live.
  • 3 Product approval: Each listing is screened for accuracy, originality, and compliance with our prohibited items policy. Mass-produced, drop-shipped, and counterfeit items are rejected.
  • 4 Ongoing monitoring: Vendor accounts are monitored for unusual activity, customer complaints, and policy violations. We conduct periodic re-verification of high-volume sellers.

Buyer protection

Purchase protection

If your order doesn't arrive, arrives damaged, or doesn't match its description, we'll make it right — every time.

Secure payments

All transactions are processed through Stripe or PayPal with full TLS encryption. Your financial details never touch our servers.

Free returns

30-day free returns on eligible items. Lahar covers the return shipping label — no restocking fees, no surprises.

Dispute handling

Our mediation team resolves disputes between buyers and vendors fairly. We handle chargeback claims so vendors don't lose earnings to fraudulent disputes.

Authenticity

Every item is vetted for originality

Lahar does not accept mass-produced, drop-shipped, or counterfeit items. Our curation team manually reviews product listings for accuracy, originality, and quality. We require vendors to submit original photos and detailed descriptions. Any listing found to violate our authenticity standards is immediately removed, and repeat violations result in permanent account suspension. We take intellectual property claims seriously and respond to DMCA takedown notices within 24 hours.

Reporting concerns

See something that doesn't feel right?

If you encounter a listing, vendor, or user that violates our policies — whether it's a counterfeit product, suspicious behaviour, harassment, or any other concern — report it to our safety team. We review all reports within 24 hours and take appropriate action, including removing listings, suspending accounts, and involving law enforcement when necessary.

Report to safety@laharhub.com

Reports can be submitted anonymously. We never share your identity with the reported party.
