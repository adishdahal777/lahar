Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies on Lahar.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

What are cookies?

Small text files that make the web work better

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, understand how you use the platform, and deliver relevant content. Some cookies are essential for the platform to function — without them, features like your shopping cart and account login wouldn't work. Others help us improve your experience by showing us which features are most useful.

Types of cookies we use

Essential

Required for operation

Authentication tokens, CSRF protection, cart state, checkout flow, and security features. These cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality. Stored for the duration of your session or up to 30 days for "remember me" preferences.

Functional

Remember your preferences

Language selection, currency preference, region, saved address details, theme choice (light/dark mode), and dismissed notification banners. These make your experience consistent across visits. Persist for up to 12 months.

Analytics

Help us improve

Page views, click tracking, scroll depth, session recordings (anonymised), and error monitoring. Powered by Google Analytics and our internal telemetry. Data is aggregated and anonymised. Persist for up to 24 months.

Marketing

Relevant recommendations

Product recommendations, personalised feeds, retargeting pixels for social media, and campaign attribution. We limit data sharing to anonymised segments. Persist for up to 90 days unless you opt out.

Third-party cookies

Stripe & PayPal

Used for payment processing and fraud detection. These are essential cookies — without them we cannot process transactions. Stripe and PayPal set their own cookies according to their respective privacy policies.

Google Analytics

Usage analytics to help us understand how visitors interact with Lahar. Data is anonymised and aggregated. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Social platforms

Embedded social sharing buttons and product pins may set cookies from Instagram, Pinterest, and TikTok. These are only activated when you interact with the sharing features.

Your choices

You have full control over cookies. Most web browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies — available in your browser's privacy settings
  • Delete existing cookies — clearing your browsing data will remove all stored cookies
  • Set cookie preferences per site — most browsers let you whitelist or blacklist specific domains
  • Use private/incognito mode — cookies are automatically deleted when you close the window

Important: Disabling essential cookies will prevent core features of Lahar from working — including login, cart, and checkout. We recommend keeping essential cookies enabled and managing only analytics and marketing cookies.
