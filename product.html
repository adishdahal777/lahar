The Pebble Pro is the answer to a quiet question Sundry has been asked for two years: what would the original Pebble buds sound like if we doubled the driver size, swapped in a beryllium-coated diaphragm, and finally — finally — committed to a four-mic ANC array?

This. They sound like this.

"They're the first true wireless I've reviewed where I forgot which playlist I was on — and just sat with the music for a while." — Sōundscape Magazine

Three sizes of memory-foam tips, a USB-C wireless charging case, multi-point pairing across two devices, and a battery that genuinely makes it through a transatlantic flight with ANC on. Made in a small facility in upstate New York, assembled by twelve people on first-name terms.

This product is hand-finished. Each pair leaves the bench with the technician's initials etched into the inside of the case.
